About Us

CHINA BODA TECHNICAL GROUP CO.LTD

China Boda Technology Group Co. Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") is a production and sales enterprise legally registered and established in Tanzania. The main business of the Company is the production and sales of regenerated lead and lead-acid batteries.

BRANCHES IN TANZANIA

China Boda serves customers through its various branches in major cities such as Dar es Salaam, Arusha, Dodoma, Mbeya and Mwanza. The company offers a wide range of car batteries, battery replacement services, and customer advice on the best batteries for their vehicles.
